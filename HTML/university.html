About Eskişehir Technical University

Welcome to Our University

"Established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018, Eskişehir Technical University is located in the heart of Eskişehir, a city of science, culture, and youth, with 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 School, and 3 Institutes, as well as 6 Research and Application Centers. Eskişehir Technical University, with its strong infrastructure where it conducts its educational and research activities, works to prepare its students for the future as the most equipped and competitive individuals, with competent academic staff who follow developments in their fields and share their time with their students. The operation of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport, located in our university's 2nd September Campus, is carried out by our Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences. Hasan Polatkan Airport hosts international flights operated by various airlines on different days of the week."


Our Mission

Our university is committed to providing students with a quality education and contributing to the advancement of science and technology, nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.


Academic Excellence

Our university is supported by a nationally and internationally recognized academic staff. Various laboratories, workshops, and project areas are available to provide students with not only theoretical knowledge but also practical experience.


Student Development

Eskişehir Technical University not only focuses on the academic success of students but also supports their personal and professional development. Services such as student clubs, social activities, and career counseling enable our students to gain a broad perspective and develop leadership qualities.


Research and Innovation

Furthermore, our university plays a significant role in research and innovation. By promoting scientific research and fostering industrial collaborations, we contribute to societal and economic development.
